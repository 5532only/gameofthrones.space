Game Overview

In gameofthrones.space, you’re dropped into a monster-infested arena. Collect weapons, level up your hero, and see how long you can withstand the horde!

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move.
  • Click or tap to shoot in the direction you aim.
  • Gather health packs and gold dropped by defeated monsters.

Features

  • Endless waves of uniquely challenging monsters.
  • Dynamic leveling system with weapon upgrades.
  • Custom skins and global leaderboard.
